About Us


Bun Bun Bake Shop is all organic bakery that focuses on making cinnamon rolls. We get our ingredients delivered every morning and only use fresh fruits and wheats.

Bun Bun was first found by Qwerty Uiop on 1989 and still continuing the ongoing project of providing our customers most fresh cinnamon rolls.

We offer flavors other than original cinnamon rolls that you are most familiar with: Blackberry, walnut, pumpkin spice, and caramel pecan.
We of course have original flavor and gluten-free original as well.

Price varies depending on how many rolls you buy at a time.
Getting more rolls at once will benefit you more!
